There are many mail merge add-ons available out there. This short guide will help you to pick the best mail merge extension to replace Yet Another Mail Merge (YAMM).

Yet Another Mail Merge is a well-known add-on to run a mail merge in Gmail. It was first launched a decade ago and became one of the leading mail merge applications over time. Yet Another Mail Merge is a product of the Revevol group, a company running several Google apps which takes care of YAMM support.

On the other hand, Mailmeteor became the best-rated mail merge addon for Gmail in three years of existence.
