Get started

Send your first mail merge campaign with Mailmeteor.

Write a draft in Gmail

In Gmail, compose the draft that will be used as template for the campaign

  • Add personalized tags in your draft with brackets, example : {{firstname}}
  • You can personnalize email subject and content, as much as you wish

Add contacts in Gsheets

In Google sheets, create a spreadsheet containing your recipients list

  • Add one colum with your contacts emails.
  • Column titles must match your draft variables to be used

Preview emails and send

  • Mailmeteor allows you to preview each emails that will be sent

FAQs

Is Mailmeteor free?

You can send up to 100 emails a day with Mailmeteor for free. We've doubled the free quota compared to existing mail merge solutions. If you want to benefit from premium features and unlock your sending limit, you can upgrade your account on our paid plans.

How is Mailmeteor different from other mailing solutions?

Contrary to email marketing softwares (like Mailchimp) Mailmeteor lets you send mail merge right from your Gmail inbox. As if you actually write each and every emails. It means better email deliverability, less spams and way better opening rate! You could also compare Mailmeteor to other mail merge extensions for Gmail. Mailmeteor is different because it's built on the latest technologies released by Google. It makes Mailmeteor way faster than any mail merge addon.

How do you avoid spam folders?

Mailmeteor directly sends emails from your Gmail account, it prevents your email falling into spams. However, please be sure to abide by Google anti-spam policies:
  • Warm up your Gmail account. If your account is new, we recommend sending emails to a small list of recipients at first (less than 50 emails).
  • Make sure your contact list is clean. If your campaigns has a lot of bounced or undelivered emails, Google will detect that your database is outdated or not legitimate. We strongly recommended you to keep your database as clean as possible. If we detect spamming behavior, we will have to block your account.
  • Insert unsubscribe links in your emails if you are running marketing campaigns.

To what extent can I personalize my emails fields?

The only limit is your imagination! You can personalize every part of the email, from subject to content. Some examples:
  • Personalized email subject.
  • Personalized greetings and recipients name. Depending on how close you are with recipients : "Hi", "Hello" or "Ghostbusters, whatya want?" for example...
  • Personalized content: add personal notes, custom URLs, postscriptum...

How do you keep my data safe?

By design, your data stays at all times on your Google Spreadsheet. Here's all the data we need in order to make Mailmeteor work:
  • Your email address - to know who you are
  • If you are a paying customer, your name
  • Campaigns sent metadata (sheet's name, date of sending - that's all)
  • Emails sent metadata (email ID in Gmail, date of sending, open / clicks events - that's all again)

How do I access email tracking stats?

Mailmeteor is able to add a tracking status column to your sheet after sending. You can access all the stats directly from there, or open the Mailmeteor sidebar for more detailed stats.

Can I add people in CC or BCC?

Sure you can. Add a column in your sheet with "cc" or "bcc" as header.
